Discover Pokhara

The Heart of Nepal

Welcome to Pokhara, a city that perfectly blends natural beauty with adventure. Nestled in the lap of the Annapurna Range, Pokhara offers visitors a unique opportunity to explore pristine lakes, lush greenery, and the majestic Himalayan peaks.

Top Attractions

  • Fewa Lake: A serene lake surrounded by the Annapurna Range, perfect for boating and picnicking.
  • World Peace Pagoda: A stunning Buddhist stupa with panoramic views of the city and mountains.
  • Phewa Tal (Phewa Lake): A tranquil freshwater lake known for its beautiful sunsets.
  • Gupteshwor Cave: An impressive limestone cave with a hidden Shiva temple.
  • Sarangkot: A hilltop offering stunning sunrise and sunset views over the Himalayas.

Cultural Experiences

  • Paragliding: Enjoy a thrilling paragliding experience over the stunning landscape.
  • Local Cuisine: Savor authentic Nepali dishes like dhido (a local dumpling), achar (pickles), and gundruk (fermented leafy greens).
  • Handicrafts: Explore vibrant markets like Lakeside for unique souvenirs, including thangka paintings, Tibetan crafts, and local jewelry.

Why Visit Pokhara?

Pokhara is not just a gateway to adventure; it's a place where you can connect with nature, enjoy thrilling activities, and witness some of the most spectacular views of the Himalayas. Whether you're an adrenaline junkie, a nature lover, or a history enthusiast, Pokhara promises an unforgettable experience.

Plan your trip to Pokhara today and experience the beauty of Nepal!
