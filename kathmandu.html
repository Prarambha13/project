Discover Kathmandu

The Heart of Nepal

Welcome to Kathmandu, a city that perfectly blends ancient heritage with vibrant modernity. Nestled in the lap of the Himalayas, Kathmandu offers visitors a unique opportunity to explore centuries-old culture, breathtaking architecture, and warm Nepali hospitality.

Top Attractions

  • Durbar Square: A UNESCO World Heritage Site filled with ancient palaces, temples, and courtyards.
  • Swayambhunath (Monkey Temple): A sacred stupa offering panoramic views of the city.
  • Pashupatinath Temple: One of the holiest Hindu temples dedicated to Lord Shiva.
  • Boudhanath Stupa: A massive Buddhist shrine that attracts pilgrims and visitors worldwide.
  • Garden of Dreams: A serene oasis in the city's heart, perfect for relaxation.

Cultural Experiences

  • Festivals: Experience vibrant festivals like Dashain, Tihar, and Indra Jatra.
  • Local Cuisine: Savor authentic Nepali dishes like momo (dumplings), dal bhat (lentil soup with rice), and Newari delicacies.
  • Handicrafts: Explore markets like Thamel for unique souvenirs, including prayer flags, singing bowls, and traditional art.

Why Visit Kathmandu?

Kathmandu is more than just a gateway to the Himalayas; it's a living museum of history, culture, and spirituality. Whether you're a history buff, a spiritual seeker, or an adventurer, this city promises an unforgettable journey.

Plan your trip to Kathmandu today and experience the magic of Nepal!
